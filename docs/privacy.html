Privacy Policy

NightTap Records Automation is a private internal tool. It connects only to a TikTok account owned by NightTap Records in order to publish video content owned by NightTap Records.

No personal data belonging to third parties is collected, stored, or shared. The only data processed is video files and captions authored by NightTap Records, and OAuth tokens used solely to authenticate the app's own TikTok account connection.

Data is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than publishing NightTap Records' own content.
